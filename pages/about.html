About me

Hi, I’m Daisy! A real estate virtual assistant with 4 years of solid experience supporting agents behind the scenes. From managing listings and contracts to creating social media content that actually gets engagement, I’ve been the go-to helping hand for realtors who want to stay focused on what they do best: selling homes and serving clients.

I’ve worked with growing teams, busy solo agents, and everything in between. I know what it’s like to juggle deadlines, deals, and DMs all in one day and that’s exactly where I come in.

If you're looking for someone reliable, easy to work with, and genuinely invested in helping you grow, you're in the right place.
